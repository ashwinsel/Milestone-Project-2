Instuctions to Play
Welcome to our Hinduism-themed quiz! This engaging experience offers you an opportunity to delve into the depths of Hindu mythology and test your knowledge in an interactive manner. Embark on a journey of discovery and exploration as you navigate through a series of questions designed to challenge and entertain you. Let's begin this enriching adventure together and see how much you know about Hinduism's rich heritage and mythology.
  1. Click on "Music On" in the top right-hand corner to start the music.
  2. Select your answer by clicking on the option you believe is correct.
  3. If your answer is correct, it will turn green; otherwise, it will turn red.
  4. If your answer is wrong, the correct answer will be highlighted in green.
  5. Click on the "Next" button, located at the bottom, to proceed to the next question.
  6. After answering 10 questions, you will receive your score.
  7. To reset the game and play again, click on the "Reset" button in the top right-hand corner.
  8. To leave this guide, click on the 'X' in the top right corner of this box.
  9. Enjoy the quiz and learn!
